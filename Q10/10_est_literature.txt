Contains 1 table with the following variables:
a) inv: Estimated number of inversions 
b) transl: Estimated number of reciprocal translocations
c) species: branch for which the estimation was done (either Euarchontoglires - Homo sapiens or Euarchontoglires - Mus musculus)
d) paper: source for the data. Either a paper as indicated, or own methods called "Stat. Est." and "Num. Est." as indicated in Master thesis.
